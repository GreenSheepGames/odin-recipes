Appelpaj

Appel means apple and paj means pie. Put them together and you have a scrumptious Swedish desert. This pie is so simple, you don't even need to make a crust. It is delicious warm or cold. For a special treat, top a slice with vanilla ice cream or whipped cream. Yum!

Ingredients:

  • 4-5 medieum apples, peeled, cored, and sliced.
  • 1 and 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp cinnamon
  • dash of cardamom
  • 3/4 cup butter
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 egg

Steps:

  1. First, preheat the oven to 350F. Then put the apple slices in the pie dish.
  2. Combine 1/4 cup of the sugar along with the cinnamon and cardamom and sprinkle it over the apples. Gently toss the apples to coat them evenly with sugar and spice.
  3. Put the butter in a medium-sized bowl and have a grownup help melt it in the microwave.
  4. Take the melted butter out of the microwave and add trhe remaining cup of sugar and the flour. Stir to combine, and then mix in the egg.
  5. Pour the mixture into the pie pan. Use the back of a spoon to spread the mixture over the apples.
  6. Bake for 45 minutes. That's it! It's easy as pie!
